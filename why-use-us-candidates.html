Why use us - candidates

Find jobs using number of years PQE

Using just one filter, it is quick and easy to see see all job vacancies at that level. You won't miss out on any new opportunities

Applications are sent directly to hiring companies

Lack of transparency is one of the biggest gripes accountants have with recruiters. By applying directly to the hiring company, you stay in control of the process

Stand out from the crowd

Hiring managers can receive 300 applications for one vacancy so finding quality applicants is like finding a needle in a haystick. As calxx is a specialised job search platform for chartered accountants, fewer applications are sent

Only deal with one job platform

Our aim is to be the one go to job search platform for chartered accountants. Instead of monitoring multiple job boards, meeting countless recruiters, you can visit calxx and see all available jobs at any one time. Think of what you can do with all the time you will save

You are in charge

As there is full transparency of what jobs are available at any one time, you decide which job you should apply for. You don't need to wait for someone to ring you up with your dream job

Be treated as a person, not a number

Hiring managers can be overwhelmed by the number of applications they receive. Unfortunately the current trend is to send generic emails to candidates saying 'due to the volume of applicants we cannot give you feedback'. We ask all hiring managers to feedback on every application.

Always look for your next role

. Usually candidates feel overwhelmed with the thought of trying to find a new job. They wait until their situation is so unbearable and then take the first job that comes along. If you are always looking for your next role, you will focus on building your career and skill set which will prove invaluable in the future. calxx makes it easy for candidates to constantly keep their eye on the job market

Only hiring companies can advertise on calxx

You can assured that the roles advertised on calxx are live opportunities
