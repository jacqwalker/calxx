Why use us - candidates

We are not a recruitment agency

calxx is a platform to connect hiring managers and chartered accountants. Companies advertise their roles on calxx and candidates apply directly. calxx was started by an ACA so we know how overwhelming and difficult the job search process can be. Our aim is to make it simpler, better, easier

Search for jobs using number of years PQE

By selecting the number of years PQE, you will quickly be able to see all vacancies at that level. You won't need to set up job alerts for different job titles and you don't need to feel like you could be missing out on potential opportunities if your search is not set up correctly

Receive feedback on every application

Unfortunately the current trend is to either not contact candidates if they decide not to proceed with them, or send a generic 'we won't be proceeding with you' email which provides no feedback to the candidate. We ask hiring managers to feedback on every application

Stand out from the crowd

Hiring managers can receive hundreds of applications for one vacancy so finding quality applicants is like searching for a needle in a haystick. Our application process ensures only the right applications get through so that you will be noticed

Applications are sent to the hiring company

We are not a recruitment agency so we have no involvement in the application process. Your application is sent directly to the hiring company and you will have full transparency and control in the process. Only hiring companies can advertise on calxx so all job opportunities are real and live.

Only deal with one job platform

Our aim is to be the go to job search platform for chartered accountants. Instead of monitoring multiple job boards, meeting countless recruiters, you can visit calxx and see all available jobs at any one time

Always be looking for your next role

We believe you should always be looking for your next role as opportunities don't always present themselves when you need them. calxx makes it easy for you to keep your eye on the job market

Simple application forms

If you have ever applied for roles using an Application Tracking System, you know how tedious it is. Our application forms are very quick and easy to complete and will save you time

Our blog

Our articles will help you build and manage your career. From productivity hacks to workplace issues, we have it covered
