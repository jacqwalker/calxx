Why use us - candidates

number_ONE

Jobs are filtered based on the number of years PQE so finding relevant jobs has never been easier

Ever had that sinking feeling when a job alert pops up in your inbox with '65 new jobs for financial controller'? Quickly followed by '25 new jobs for FP&A' because you do not know what criteria to set your job alerts or search to?

We take that pain away. By looking at the number of years PQE, you can quickly identify all available jobs at that level. No more wondering if you should search for 'financial controller' or 'FP&A' or 'Finance Business Partner' or any other variation on job title which your ideal role might be hiding under. And as you are looking at the right number of years PQE, you will no longer be applying for jobs which are too junior or senior for you

number_TWO

Your application is sent directly to the hiring company

Other job boards do not have screening techniques which means hiring managers can receive up to 300 applications for any job posting. Its like finding a needle in a haystack. You don't want your application lost amongst that. With our screening techniques, hiring managers receive a lower number of applications, but the quality of applications is signifcantly higher. We ask all hiring managers to give feedback on each application, whether successful or not, as we believe it is important to remember the human element in the recruitment process: to treat each candidate as a human, not a number. The generic 'due to the high volume of applications we cannot give individual feedback on your application' response to your application email is a thing of the past

number_THREE

Only deal with one job platform

Instead of having to look at multiple online job boards, sign up with multiple recruiters, waste time meeting said recruiters, you can visit calxx and see all available jobs at any one time. This makes the job search simpler, better and easier and all the time you save can be spent on really fun activities. You are also in control as to how you are represented to the hiring manager and you get to decide which roles you want to apply for.

number_FOUR

You are in charge of your career

We believe you should always be looking for your next role. What tends to happen is candidates feel overwhelmed with the thought of trying to find a new job, that they wait until their situation is so unbearable and then take the first job that comes along. Jumping out of the fire into the frying pan so to speak. If candidates are constantly looking for their next role, they will focus on building their career and skill set which will prove invaluable in the future. calxx makes it easy for candidates to constantly keep their eye on the job market
