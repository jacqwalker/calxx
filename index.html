A job platform for chartered accountants

calxx is a job platform that connects hiring managers with ACA qualified accountants. Started by an ACA, our aim is to be the only place that all ACA qualified accountants go to, whether as a hiring manager or candidate, to have their recruitment needs met.

Not just a better recruitment experience. It's the recruitment experience.

Hiring managers

You’ve had that dreaded resignation. Now what? Well you log onto calxx, advertise your vacancy, pay a fixed fee and before you know it, you’re interviewing quality candidates. The hardest part is having to decide between candidates as they all so good ... you almost wish you had more vacancies ... almost.

calxx is for the hiring manager that wants a recruitment process that is so good, so simple, so easy. And all at a fixed fee

How it works:

advertise on our platform to target ACA / CA qualified candidates

our online screening does the work that recruiters normally do so only relevant applications are sent to your inbox

you and the candidate deal directly
