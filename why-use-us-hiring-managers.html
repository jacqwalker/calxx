Why use us - Hiring Managers

We are not a recruitment agency

calxx is a platform to connect hiring managers and chartered accountants. You advertise your role on calxx and candidates apply directly. You have full control of the process

Cheaper, cost-effective recruitment process

There is no cost to advertise your first role with us. Thereafter its a flat monthly fee, so you are not charged per application or view

Quality applications

We work with you to tailor the application form so you only receive quality and relevant applications. If you prefer to receive hundreds of applications to whittle down to a handful, then calxx is not for you as we believe in simpler, better, easier

Don't lose out on top talent

The applications you receive are very easy to review so you can quickly decide who to interview. You no longer need to worry about losing out on good candidates because you are overwhelmed with the number of applications

Personal touch

calxx was started by an ACA so we have a huge amount of experience to draw on. If you would like a sounding board on the applications you receive, we are here to help at no additional cost

Build your talent pipeline

We believe companies should always be looking to hire. As you will receive top notch applications, building your talent pipeline has never been easier
